About Bindr

We help people find their perfect business match.

Bindr is a professional networking app built for the people LinkedIn forgot — students looking for their first internship, founders raising their first round, and professionals ready for their next chapter.

Our mission

Connecting the business community with their perfect match.

Our vision

Creating sustainable relationships between users.

Our story

Bindr started in 2024 with a simple observation: every professional networking platform we used felt like a job board, a contact list, or a feed — never a real introduction. Founders were buried under cold DMs, students were lost in posting forums, and mentors couldn't find each other through a sea of profiles.

We built Bindr to feel different. A swipe interface puts you in control. Deep, structured profiles tell people exactly who you are and what you're looking for. And a shared community keeps the whole experience grounded in real outcomes — not vanity metrics.

Today Bindr has 4,800+ active profiles in 38 countries — students, freelancers, founders, hiring teams, investors, and the schools that connect them. We're just getting started.
